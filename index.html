Sir Alex Ferguson is the most successful manager in British football history, winning 13 Premier League titles with Manchester United. Having spent all of his playing career in Scotland, Ferguson first went into management with East Stirlingshire in 1974 before joining St Mirren in the same year.

This website will show you the facts and statics from Sir Alex's managerial career. These facts will explain why he is one of the greatest managers of all time. Some people don't like him because of the clubs he has managed, but there are not many people who don't rate him highly.

If you feel like there are better managers than Sir Alex, feel free to give me your suggestions / opinions via the contact page.
